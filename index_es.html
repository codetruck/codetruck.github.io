The CodeTruck

The Code Truck is a social enterprise focused on generating programming skills in young people from urban slums. Our objective is for them to improve their quality of life and to reduce the digital divide. We do this through a stepwise and collaborative learning strategy; a scalable model of mobile units, where every codetruck functions as a training center and a business incubator; and by setting up a freelance web development platform.

Our Motivation

The use of technology has enhanced the scope of our creative capacity. However, it has also dramatically increased inequality in the world. The digital divide does not allow the talent of entire communities be detected and boosted, and most schools do not have the resources to provide such opportunities to young people.

Acercamiento

We want to get to the right places so our first step is to identify the colonies that meet socio-economic criteria that make them ideal candidates for the project. We will approach them on weekends, in mobile units, organizing simple computer related activities to attract people and invite them to the next stage of the project.



Identifying Talent

Los jóvenes serán invitados a cursos y certificaciones de fin de semana en herramientas tecnológicas mediante plataformas online (MOOC's) de capacitación como PLATZI, esto nos permitirá identificar a individuos con capacidad, motivación y compromiso, atributos que dificilmente son capturados en exámenes. Estos jóvenes serán invitados a nuestro proceso intensivo de capacitación.

Capacitación Productiva

We will teach our students a diversity of programming and management skills throughout a project based collaborative learning scheme. The training process will be divided in three staggered stages in terms of complexity and each one will have duration of two months. We want to play the role of a business incubator, meaning that our students will work as a team during the traing process with the potential to form an enterprise at the end. Moreover, we want to nurture our students with the necessary capabilities to become economically independent and to provide them better perspectives towards the future.

Vinculación Laboral

In order to build solid opportunities for our graduates, we'll build an online freelance platform for linking students with potential clients. We receive and assign projects through the platform, charging a small commission for graduates to ensure Codetrucks financial stability.
